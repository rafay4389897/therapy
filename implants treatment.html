What Are Implants?

Implants are titanium screws that are used to replace the roots of your missing teeth. They are used to support crowns, bridges or dentures. Implants are surgically placed in your jawbone. Most of the time, implants feel more natural and secure than any other methods of replacing missing teeth.

How Much Time Is Required For Implants?

The procedure is performed in two steps.

STEP 1 (Implant Placement) – When you decide to come in for placing your implants its is a short quick 30-min surgery under complete sterilization, after numbing the area, the gum is exposed, and a small drill helps place the implant into your jawbone.

Following the placement of the implants our doctors may place resorbable sutures and then you would be asked to visit us 5-6 days after the surgery for a follow-up, at home do expect mild to moderate swelling, but with good antibiotics, the pain and swelling can be kept to a minimum. The implant is left untouched for about 3-6 months to allow it to take hold within the bone, you can continue with your normal daily routine

STEP 2 (Crown Placement) – After 3-6 months we will call you back into our office to take a few measurement impressions, these may take 2/3 appointments depending on the complexity but plan this window to be about a 2-week period.

We shall fabricate the best possible teeth, meeting all best international specifications, bringing you the perfect smile that you shall be exceptionally proud off

How Much Do Implants Cost?

The cost for having tooth dental implants will depend on several factors, including the number of teeth being replaced and the number of implants required to support your replacement teeth.

You are welcome to come in for a FREE detailed consultation where the specialist doctor will evaluate your case and guide you accordingly as to the costs involved. Avail your first FREE CONSULTATION NOW.

Will Dental Implants Work For Me?

Anyone who is missing one or more teeth. The main limitation is the amount of available bone. Our doctors can help evaluate whether dental implants are a viable solution for you

What If I’m Travelling & Not In Karachi

Dental Tourism in Pakistan is a flourishing industry, people come to Karachi get implants placed and that is cheaper than getting the implants placed in many parts of the world like US, UK, Canada or Europe.

If you are a traveling patient, correspond with our team via email hospital@alvidental.com or call us on WhatsApp.

STEP 1: People who are travelling need to come in for 1/2 days for the implant placement surgery.

STEP 2: Patients can return to Pakistan any time after 3 months and we need to have you here for about 1-2 weeks for the crown placement on top of the implant.
