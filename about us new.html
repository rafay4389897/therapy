More About Us
Formation of Merlin Entertainments

`Merlin Entertainments was formed in 1999 when the management team of Vardon Attractions, a subsidiary of Vardon PLC, purchased the company

Expansion and Acquisitions

`Over the years, Merlin Entertainments expanded its portfolio through various acquisitions. In 2005, it was bought by Blackstone Group, a private equity firm. Subsequent acquisitions included the Tussauds Group, LEGOLAND parks, Sea Life Centres, and various other attractions.

London Eye and Other Landmarks

Merlin Entertainments acquired the London Eye, a famous landmark in London, in 2007. This iconic observation wheel has become a significant tourist attraction under the company's management

Innovations and Collaborations

The company consistently focused on innovation and collaborations with popular franchises and brands to introduce new and exciting attractions. Collaborations with LEGO, Marvel, and other well-known brands led to the creation of themed experiences and rides.
